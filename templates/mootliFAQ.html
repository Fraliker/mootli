What is Mootli?

Mootli is a mobile app that connects you to local people and events that match your interests and schedule.

How does it work?

Browse for hangouts in your local area.
Join any hangout instantly.
Create new hangouts in 60 seconds or less.
Browsing, Joining, and Creating hangouts is completely free.

What's in it for me?

Long day at work? Create a hangout to grab a drink and relax with like-minded locals.

Can't find enough people to join you for a boardgame night? Create a hangout to let local boardgamers know.

At a convention and don't know anyone? Create a hangout to create your own small group to hang with.

Interested locals can easily join you with the tap of a button!

Where is it available?

Mootli is now available in the San Francisco bay area with more cities coming soon.

About Mootli

Mootli's core value is happiness. Our mission is very simple. Connecting you to people you want to be connected with. We founded Mootli to help people to do more of what they love. We are gamers, golfers, and car geeks who were tired of weekends spent looking for people to join us at the table, on the course, or at the garage.

Get Started

1 Log-in

You can sign up to Mootli by connecting to Facebook. Logging in allows you to create and join new Hangouts.



2 Browse

Browse hangouts by scrolling down. Hangouts are listed in the order in which they are created. Tap any hangout to see more details.



3 Join

Choose Join to join any hangout with available spots. You can also leave a hangout that you've already joined.



4 Create

If you can't find exactly what you're looking for, you can create a hangout in 60 seconds or less by choosing the "+Create" button. Fill in some quick details about your hangout, tap create and your hangout will appear in the main page for others to join.
