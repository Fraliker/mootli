Why Mootli?

Mootli's core value is happiness. Our mission is very simple. Connecting you to people you want to be connected with.

What is Mootli?

Mootli is a mobile app to help you find nearby, like-minded friends wherever you go. Instantly.

Example?

Long day at work? Create a hangout to grab a drink and vent with like-minded locals.

Can't find enough people to join you for a boardgame night? Create a hangout to let local boardgamers know.

At a convention and don't know anyone? Create a hangout to create your own small group to hang with.

Interested locals will see your hangout instantly and easily join you with the tap of a button!
